About Us

SamSonRepas Inc. was founded by Christopher Samson, a passionate food enthusiast with years of experience in the food industry. Christopher’s journey began at Burger King, where he gained valuable insights into fast-paced restaurant operations, customer service excellence, and the importance of consistency and quality.

Inspired by his heritage and a deep love for African cuisine, Christopher traveled to Africa to immerse himself in the rich culinary traditions of the continent. During his travels, he learned authentic cooking techniques and recipes firsthand, deepening his appreciation for the vibrant flavors and cultural significance of African food.

His passion is also fueled by his wife, who is of African descent, and whose family recipes and traditions inspire many of the dishes served at SamSonRepas Inc. Together, they blend family heritage and professional experience to bring delicious, comforting meals to their community.

At SamSonRepas, we also celebrate the multicultural spirit of Canada by including classic Canadian favorites like juicy steaks, hearty poutine, and sweet butter tarts on our menu. This unique combination of African and Canadian cuisines allows us to offer a diverse dining experience that caters to many tastes.

Christopher believes food is more than nourishment — it’s a way to connect people, celebrate culture, and share stories. With SamSonRepas, he offers dishes made from fresh, local ingredients that feel like home.

Thanks to Christopher’s dedication and the support of his team, SamSonRepas has become a beloved community spot known for authentic flavors and warm service. Christopher looks forward to welcoming you and sharing the tastes of Africa and Canada with every meal.
