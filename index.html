Welcome to SamSonRepas Inc.

Experience the rich flavors of authentic African cuisine blended with the finest Canadian dishes. Founded by Christopher Samson, who brings years of culinary experience from working at Burger King and traveling across Africa, SamSonRepas offers a unique fusion that delights every palate. Christopher’s wife is of African descent, which adds deep cultural authenticity to every dish served.

About Us

Christopher Samson started his culinary journey working at Burger King, gaining valuable skills in fast-paced kitchen environments. His passion for food led him to travel extensively across Africa, learning traditional recipes and cooking techniques firsthand. With a vision to bring authentic African dishes to Canada, he founded SamSonRepas Inc., a restaurant that proudly serves both African and Canadian favorites like steak.

The influence of his African-descended wife inspires the menu’s authenticity and warmth, ensuring every plate tells a story. Our mission is to share culture through cuisine and provide an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates diversity.
