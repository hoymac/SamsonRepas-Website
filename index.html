About Us

Christopher Samson, our founder, brings over a decade of food service experience, having worked at Burger King in Canada. His passion for food led him to travel extensively across Africa, immersing himself in the culinary traditions and mastering authentic recipes. Inspired by his African-descended wife, SamSonRepas Inc. was born to celebrate the rich flavors and culture of Africa while embracing Canadian favorites. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space where diverse communities can come together and enjoy exceptional meals crafted with love and care.

At SamSonRepas Inc., we pride ourselves on delivering authentic flavors that bring people together. From traditional recipes handed down through generations to modern twists on Canadian classics, every dish is crafted with care and passion. We believe food is more than nourishment — it's a way to share culture, stories, and happiness.

Menu

Our menu showcases a diverse selection of dishes that bring together the rich flavors of African culinary traditions and beloved Canadian favorites. Every item is prepared fresh daily using high-quality ingredients, designed to satisfy your appetite and delight your taste buds.
