Welcome to SamSonRepas Inc.

Experience the authentic taste of African cuisine blended with Canadian favorites in the heart of Gatineau, QC. Our founder Christopher Samson brings years of experience from working at Burger King and has traveled extensively across Africa to master traditional dishes. With a wife of African descent inspiring our flavors, we proudly serve a menu that celebrates rich heritage and fresh local ingredients. Our carefully crafted dishes include time-honored recipes like Jollof Rice, Suya, Egusi Soup, and Canadian classics like Poutine with a unique twist. We emphasize fresh, local produce and spices imported directly from Africa to ensure authenticity in every bite. We are committed to providing a welcoming and vibrant environment where families, friends, and food lovers from all backgrounds can come together to enjoy delicious meals and cultural experiences. Join us for lunch, dinner, or special events celebrating African culture through food, music, and community.

About Us

Christopher Samson, our founder, brings over a decade of food service experience, having worked at Burger King in Canada. His passion for food led him to travel extensively across Africa, immersing himself in the culinary traditions and mastering authentic recipes. Inspired by his African-descended wife, SamSonRepas Inc. was born to celebrate the rich flavors and culture of Africa while embracing Canadian favorites. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space where diverse communities can come together and enjoy exceptional meals crafted with love and care.

At SamSonRepas Inc., we believe food is a powerful connector that brings people together. We take pride in our commitment to sourcing fresh, local ingredients while honoring traditional African culinary techniques and flavors. Our team works passionately to create dishes that tell a story—one of heritage, family, and celebration.

Beyond serving delicious food, we actively engage with the community through cultural events, cooking classes, and collaborations with local artists and musicians. We are proud to contribute to the vibrant cultural mosaic of Gatineau and invite you to join us in this journey.

Thank you for choosing SamSonRepas Inc. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing the tastes and traditions that inspire us.
