From spicy Suya skewers to comforting Jollof rice, every bite tells a story of heritage and dedication. We are committed to using fresh, local ingredients while honoring the rich culinary traditions of Africa and Canada.

About Us

Christopher Samson, our founder, brings over a decade of experience in food service and hospitality. His journey began at Burger King in Canada and blossomed through extensive travels across Africa, immersing himself in authentic culinary traditions and recipes. Inspired by his African-descended wife and a passion for bringing people together through food, SamSonRepas Inc. was created to celebrate the rich flavors of African and Canadian cuisines. Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming environment where diverse communities can enjoy meals crafted with love, fresh ingredients, and respect for tradition.

We pride ourselves on quality, authenticity, and exceptional customer service. Whether you're craving spicy Suya skewers or comforting Canadian Poutine, our menu is designed to offer something special for every palate. Join us to taste a delicious fusion of cultures and experience the heart of SamSonRepas Inc.
