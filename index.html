Welcome to SamSonRepas!

Christopher Samson brings years of experience cooking authentic African dishes combined with Canadian favorites like steak and poutine. After traveling to Africa and learning from local chefs — with his wife being of African descent — SamSonRepas Inc. is proud to offer a unique fusion of tastes.
