About Us

Christopher Samson, founder of SamSonRepas Inc., has a deep passion for food that started with his early culinary experience working at Burger King. His journey expanded when he traveled extensively across Africa, learning traditional recipes and cooking techniques from various regions. His wife, of African descent, has also inspired many of the dishes you find on our menu today.

At SamSonRepas, we blend authentic African flavors with classic Canadian favorites to create a unique dining experience here in Gatineau, QC. Whether you're craving the spicy and vibrant tastes of Suya and Jollof Rice or the comforting familiarity of a well-cooked steak, our menu has something for everyone.

We pride ourselves on quality ingredients, warm service, and sharing our culinary heritage with the community.
