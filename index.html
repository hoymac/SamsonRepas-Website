About Us

Christopher Samson, our founder, brings over a decade of food service experience, having worked at Burger King in Canada. His passion for food led him to travel extensively across Africa, immersing himself in the culinary traditions and mastering authentic recipes. Inspired by his African-descended wife, SamSonRepas was born to celebrate the rich flavors and culture of Africa while embracing Canadian favorites. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space where diverse communities can come together and enjoy exceptional meals crafted with love and care.

At SamSonRepas, we believe food is more than just nourishment — it’s a way to connect, celebrate heritage, and share stories. Our kitchen uses only fresh, locally sourced ingredients alongside authentic spices and recipes passed down through generations. We are committed to quality, sustainability, and bringing you the best flavors from Africa and Canada.

Menu

Our menu is a vibrant fusion of traditional African delicacies and beloved Canadian classics. Each dish is carefully prepared with fresh ingredients and authentic spices to bring you an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you crave hearty soups, flavorful grilled meats, or comforting comfort foods, there’s something here for everyone.
